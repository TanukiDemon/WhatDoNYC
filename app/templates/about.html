Our Mission

Here at What Do NYC, we want to connect New Yorkers to everything New York City has to offer, from its famous sights and landmarks to its smallest, best-hidden gems.


About What Do NYC

What Do NYC is here to help you explore parts of New York City that you'll love-- without having to jump through any hoops to find them! We've streamlined the process of discovering new places to go and finding fun things to do, so you don't have to. When you make an account with us, we'll ask you a few questions to find out about who you are. Then, once we've gotten to know you, we'll give you personalized recommendations for the hottest venues and activities the city has to offer. We know New Yorkers are busy, and life moves fast, so at What Do NYC, we've cut out the searching and planning so you can get straight to the living and enjoying!
