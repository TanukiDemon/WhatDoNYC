Our Mission

What Do NYC's mission is to connect New Yorkers and tourists with all the great places in NYC.


About What Do NYC

What Do NYC hopes to introduce exciting and unique experiences that will enhance the lives of New Yorkers and tourists alike and encourage them to experience all NYC has to offer. With personalized recommendations for all of our users, we'll surely be able to suggest what you should do in NYC.
